Muhammad Ali

Picture of Muhammad Ali

Muhammad Ali is widely regarded by many boxing commentators and historians as the greatest heavyweight boxer of all time.

Boxing magazine The Ring named him number 1 in a 1998 ranking of greatest heavyweights from all eras.

In 1999, The Associated Press voted Ali the number one heavyweight of the 20th century.[4] In 1999, Ali was named the second greatest boxer in history, pound for pound, by ESPN; behind only welterweight and middleweight legend Sugar Ray Robinson.
